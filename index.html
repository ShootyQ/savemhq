What is SavemHQ?

SavemHQ is my “general domain” for whatever I’m making, testing, or daydreaming about. Sometimes it’s an app idea. Sometimes it’s a classroom thing. Sometimes it’s something for my kids. If you see half-finished bits, that’s not a bug. It’s the point.

“SAVEM” is a little family acronym, and “HQ” felt right because most of my projects start as a mission briefing and end as a side quest.
